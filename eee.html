Electrical and Electronics Engineering

Specializing in electrical circuits, power systems, and electronics, preparing students for careers in renewable energy and electronics industries.

About the Department

The Electrical and Electronics Engineering department at Syed Ammal Engineering College offers a comprehensive education in modern electrical and electronic systems. Our curriculum covers key areas such as power systems, digital electronics, control systems, and renewable energy technologies. We focus on equipping students with practical skills and theoretical knowledge, fostering innovation and research.

Courses Offered

  • Power Systems
  • Digital Electronics
  • Control Systems
  • Renewable Energy Technologies

Outcomes

  • Strong foundation in electrical and electronic systems.
  • Skills in renewable energy solutions and applications.
  • Career readiness for the energy and electronics sectors.

Research Highlights

Smart Grid Technology

Research focused on enhancing the efficiency and reliability of power distribution systems through advanced grid technologies.

Renewable Energy Integration

Exploring methods to integrate renewable energy sources into the existing power grid, promoting sustainable energy solutions.

Student Projects

Solar Energy Harvesting

Developing efficient systems for harvesting solar energy, aimed at increasing the accessibility and affordability of renewable energy.

Smart Home Automation

A project that integrates smart technologies to automate and optimize home energy consumption, enhancing comfort and efficiency.
